## SetPixel() 
This is for the `setPixel();` function

### Math
$$ 
\displaylines{
pc = \text{pixel coordinates} \\
pcx = \text{pixel x coordinates} \\
pcy = \text{pixel y coordinates} \\
px = \text{untreated x coordinates} \\
py = \text{untreated y coordinates} \\
ps = \text{size of the pixel} \\
pc = (pcx, pcy) \\
pcx = ps \times px \\
pcy = ps \times py \\
}
$$
